Our Dental Services

IMPLANT DENTISTRY

LEARN MORE

This treatment places an artificial tooth root into your jawbone to replace a missing tooth. It looks, feels, and functions like a natural tooth.

WISDOM TOOTH EXTRACTIONS

LEARN MORE

We remove wisdom teeth (third molars) that are causing pain, infection, or crowding. Our office can handle even complex extractions comfortably.

CLEAR ALIGNERS

LEARN MORE

A nearly invisible way to straighten your teeth using a series of clear, removable trays. They gradually move your teeth into the right position.

TMJ TREATMENT

LEARN MORE

We diagnose and treat problems with the jaw joint (TMJ), such as pain, clicking, or limited movement. Treatment may include a mouthguard, therapy, or medication.

EMERGENCY DENTISTRY

LEARN MORE

We provide fast care for dental emergencies like toothaches, broken teeth, injuries, or gum bleeding. Our goal is quick relief and lasting solutions.

ROOT CANAL THERAPY

LEARN MORE

A treatment to save a tooth when the nerve is infected or damaged. We clean, seal, and protect the tooth so you can keep it.

COSMETIC DENTISTRY

LEARN MORE

We help improve the look of your smile by fixing the color, shape, or alignment of your teeth. Services include whitening, veneers, and more.

RESTORATIVE DENTISTRY

LEARN MORE

We repair teeth damaged by decay, cracks, or wear. Treatments include fillings, inlays, and onlays to restore strength and function.

PREVENTATIVE DENTISTRY

LEARN MORE

We focus on keeping your mouth healthy and stopping problems before they start. This includes cleanings, check-ups, and fluoride treatments.

CROWNS & BRIDGES

LEARN MORE

Crowns cover and protect weak or broken teeth. Bridges fill the gap left by missing teeth using nearby teeth for support.

DENTURES

LEARN MORE

Custom-made removable devices that replace several or all missing teeth. They help you chew, speak, and smile confidently.

PERIODONTIC TREATMENT

LEARN MORE

We treat gum disease to stop infection and protect the bone that supports your teeth. This may include deep cleanings or gum surgery.
